Game objective: Kill everyone!!!
-help - Displays commands.
-map - Displays the map and the nemies and the LifeElexirs on it.
-left - Move left.
-right - Move right.
-up - Move up.
-down - Move down.
-status - Display Hero current health, attack and elexir inventory.
-enemies - Display enemies left.
-enemiesInfo - Displays all info about enemies - Race, health and damage.
-ElexirInfo - Display all LifeElexirs values.
-heal - Heal yourself if you have an elexir.
-clear - Clears the screen.
-retreat - You has been slain by the evil enemies and you will never see the sun light again!
